About The Groove

At The Groove, we’re redefining the clubbing scene in Los Angeles. We host events featuring local artists, creating a space where music, community, and self-expression thrive. Our mission is to break the stigma surrounding L.A. nightlife by making our events accessible—most are free, and when ticketed, we keep prices as affordable as possible.

We believe in fostering a judgment-free environment where friends can come together, dance, and truly enjoy the moment. With events twice a month and surprise pop-ups along the way, there’s always something happening.

We are always looking for new ideas and new people to join our team or roster. Feel free to email us or shoot us a message on Instagram.

Follow us on our socials and check out our website to stay updated on upcoming events. See you on the dance floor!
